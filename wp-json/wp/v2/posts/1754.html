Al Barakah Holding Receives India Ambassador<\/p>\n

Al Barakah Holding received the top diplomat at the Indian Embassy in UAE on March 22, 2017.<\/p>\n

The management met with Mr. Navdeep Singh Suri, the Indian ambassador to the UAE and accompanied him in a tour around one of our workers\u2019 accommodation villages in Mussafah, Workers Village.<\/p>\n

The Ambassador assessed the facilities and interacted with the Indian community residing at our village for years and enquiring about their whereabouts.<\/p>\n

The officials of ZonesCorp (Higher Corporation for Specialised Economic Zones that regulates the management of workers\u2019 accommodation) were also present during the visit.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
