Ramadan Kareem from Al Barakah Holding<\/p>\n

As the moon heralds the start of the holy month of Ramadan, Al Barakah Holding extends the best wishes to Muslims across the United Arab Emirates and around the world.<\/p>\n

This blessed month gives us the opportunity to focus on compassion for those less fortunate, to sympathize with those who may not have supper meal at the comfort of their homes throughout the year.<\/p>\n

During the course of fasting, the daily grace teaches us lessons in gratitude for the goodness of God, harmony in wellbeing, endurance in life path, and unity across the community.<\/p>\n
