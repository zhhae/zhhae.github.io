A Lot to Share<\/p>\n

ICCC, a subsidiary of Al Barakah Holding, was commissioned by Al Barakah International Investments and Department of Municipal Affairs to develop one of the iconic tourist destination in the Emirate of Abu Dhabi.<\/p>\n

Today, ICCC releases the 3D video of the Al Qana showcasing a glimpse of what to expect.<\/p>\n

Al Qana project is the ideal tourist and entertainment destination. It features the largest aquarium in Abu Dhabi and biggest standalone cinema in Abu Dhabi; in addition to various attractions across 5 zones. Each zone is a reflection of a unique experience starring sports, gaming, yacht club, casual and fine dining, as well as retail outlets.<\/p>\n

Located along the Khor Al Maqta area in Abu Dhabi, near Shangri-La hotel, Al Qana will be one of the new urban landmarks of UAE.<\/p>\n
