Al Saadiyat Village Receives Happiness Award<\/p>\n

Abu Dhabi \u2013 May 4, 2017: Under the patronage of Minister of Human Resources and Emiratisation, H.E. Saqr Ghobash, and Minister of State for Happiness, H.E.Ohood Bint Khalfan Al Roumi, Al Saadiyat Village was awarded the \u201cHappiest Accommodation Village\u201d during \u201cThe Happiness of Work Program\u201d event on May 2, 2017.<\/p>\n

\u201cThe Happiness of Work Program\u201d event was organized during the International Workers Day to celebrate the success of the private sector initiatives towards achieving a better living and happier environment in the United Arab Emirates.<\/p>\n

Managed by Mayar Facilities Management, Al Saadiyat Village was honored for its best living conditions and recreation facilities among the accommodation villages in the country.<\/p>\n

Mr. Fouad Mashal, CEO of Al Barakah Holding who received this awarded said in a statement: \u201cIt was truly an honor being recognized as the Happiest Accommodation Village by the government.\u201d He added \u201cWe continuously seek to achieve better living conditions for those who represent the major workforce in the country.\u201d<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
