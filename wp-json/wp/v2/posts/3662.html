As the CEO of Al Barakah International Investment, Mashal has been pivotal in driving the construction of Al Qana<\/p>\n

Another debutant in the Construction Week Power 100 list this year is Fouad Mashal, the CEO of Al Barakah International Investment, who has been pivotal in driving the construction of Abu Dhabi\u2019s up-and-coming waterfront destination Al Qana.<\/p>\n

Touted to change the UAE capital\u2019s landscape, Mousal described Al Qana as the \u201cultimate touristic project\u201d that will provide a very unique experience we all envision for the entertainment and tourism sectors in the UAE.<\/p>\n

The development recently reached 90% construction completion and is expected to be completed by Q3 2021. The destination will feature a National Aquarium, cinema complexes and offers a clear view of the Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque.<\/p>\n

Al Barakah also recently unveiled the latest addition to the Al Qana development: Al Qana Marina, which will feature restaurants, cafes and marine services.<\/p>\n

The amenities of Al Qana\u2019s Marina include 24-hour security, vessel monitoring, emergency response equipment, highly equipped professionals, as well as an extensive range of marine services and facilities.<\/p>\n<\/div>
