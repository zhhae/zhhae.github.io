Out of many workers\u2019 residential cities across the nation, the Ministry of Human Resources and Emiratisation named our facility \u201cWorkers Village\u201d the Happiest Workers\u2019 Residential City in the United Arab Emirates for 2018.<\/p>\n

On the behalf of Al Barakah Holding, the Managing Director Moataz Mashal and the Head of Business Development Abdulla Saeed bin Omeir received the accolade from H.E. Nasser bin Juma Thani Al Hamli, Minister of Human Resources and Emiratisation at the Ritz Carlton DIFC on the occasion of the International Workers\u2019 Day 2018.<\/p>\n

In an event last year, our facility \u201cSaadiyat Village\u201d received a similar award by the Ministry of Human Resources and Emiratization and Ministry of State of Happiness.
\nWe would like to thank our employees, partners, and clients for contributing to this success.<\/p>\n
