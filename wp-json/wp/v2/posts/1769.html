As part of nation-wide celebrations on the occasion of the anniversary of His Highness Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan as President of the UAE, Al Barakah Holding hoisted the UAE Flag at its Full Mast on November 2 at 11:00 am. Our participation in raising the flag of the UAE on this day stems from our belonging to the precious country and the leaders as a symbol of pride.<\/p>\n

The national anthem was played to share this special moment with all the staff and to reflect the image of belonging and loyalty to the country.<\/p>\n
