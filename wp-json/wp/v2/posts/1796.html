Al Barakah Holding organized a Pink Ribbon Event for women staff as part of its social initiatives to the international awareness campaign month for Breast Cancer.<\/p>\n

The Marketing team aimed to raise awareness and reduce the stigma of breast cancer through a brief program educating the symptoms and treatment at the Training Hall on October 24, 2018. Dr. Abeer Al Naqbi from Seha shared her knowledge about the disease, the importance of early detection, methods of self-examination, and misconceptions related to the condition.<\/p>\n

The staff were pleased with the information and were invited to schedule an appointment at Seha.<\/p>\n
