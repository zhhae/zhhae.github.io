The 45th National Day Celebration With Great Spirit<\/p>\n

Al Barakah Holding greets on the occasion of the 45th\u00a0National Day the President of UAE,\u00a0His Highness Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan, the Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and Deputy Supreme Commander of UAE,\u00a0His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, and the Vice President of UAE, Prime Minister of UAE, and Ruler of Dubai,\u00a0His Highness Sheikh Mohammad bin Rashid Al Maktoum, and the rest of their Highnesses and Rules of the Emirates.<\/p>\n

The greeting is extended to the people of this nation –\u00a0 the people of peace, growth, and generosity. On this occasion, we pay tribute to the local martyrs who sacrificed for upholding the values of our ancestors and culture and protect this united nation.<\/p>\n

For the past 44 years, one commitment by His Highness Sheikha Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan and his dear Rulers still proves to us the true meaning of unity, has enabled the transformation of UAE development and sustainability, and has shown unprecedented innovation\u00a0at all levels.<\/p>\n

Let us be as strong as this nation, as united as this nation, and as courageous as this nation. Let us build a successful partnership that comprehends the spirit of progress and development.<\/p>\n

All the praises and thanks be to the lord of the lords for blessing us with great leaders of a great nation.<\/p>\n
