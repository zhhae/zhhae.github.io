Al Barakah Holding has completed a new milestone in the journey of accomplishment and success by being awarded \u201cSuperbrands Status for Year 2018\u201d in the United Arab Emirates.<\/p>\n

This prestigious award is only granted through a strict selection process by the UAE Superbrands Council headed by the former Minister of Foreign Trade H.E. Sheikha Luban Al Qassimi, Vice Chairman of Dubai Duty Free, Vice President of Du, CEO of Dubai Shopping Festival, and over 4,000 senior marketing professionals.<\/p>\n

This is a mark that our brand reputation is getting recognized locally and internationally. Those who previously won this award are Calvin Klein, MasterCard, Pepsi, Toyota, Renault, Mont Blanc, Emaar, and many other top-tier brands.<\/p>\n
