In line with building a modernized culture, Al Barakah Holding held the staff annual event at Mashal farm. The event aimed at creating an innovative and smart learning environment for the company to be at the leading-edge of internal business development.<\/p>\n

The purpose of the slogan \u201cGo Far Together, is to deliberately promote an environment of innovation and set up strong team work and collaboration, all for achieving ingenuity together.<\/p>\n

The event featured exciting team building activities like tug of war, sack race competition, racing on plywood and many more. There were also special awards distributed among employees; ones who completed 10 years and above at the company, best performing employees and departments, best strategist and other recognitions.<\/p>\n
